About Our Institution

Sri Indu College of Engineering and Technology is a premier institution established with a vision to impart quality technical education and foster innovation. Affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, the college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Spread over a sprawling campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure, the college provides an ideal environment for academic excellence and holistic development. Our faculty comprises experienced educators and industry experts who mentor students to become future-ready professionals.

The college has a strong industry-academia interface with collaborations with leading corporations, providing students with excellent internship and placement opportunities. With a focus on research, innovation, and entrepreneurship, Sri Indu College continues to produce graduates who excel in their chosen fields.
